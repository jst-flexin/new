FACTS ABOUT FERRARI

Ferrari S.p.A is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer based in Maranello,Italy.Founded in 1939 by Enzo Ferrari ,the company built its first car in 1940,adopted its current name in 1945,and began to produce its current line of road cars in 1947.

One of the interesting facts about Ferrari is that the first model was not mass produced and only two cars were brought on the track of Piacenza Racing Circuit,driven by Nina Farina and Franco Cortese.The initial 125 Sport models had fuel pump issues on the race track but recovered fast soon thereafter.
